Cryptocurrency Exchange Script

A White Label Cryptocurrency Exchange Script is a customizable pre-built software solution that lets entrepreneurs and business persons launch their own cryptocurrency trading platform instantly without the need of developing it from scratch. It has essential features like futures trading and spot, KYC/AML compliance, multi-currency support, liquid management support, as well as other top-tier security protocols. Using this ready-made software helps companies reduce development costs while ensuring a scalable, safe, secure, and high-performance trading platform. It also supports several trading models like Decentralized (DEX), Centralized (CEX), and Peer-To-Peer (P2P) exchanges, making it a functional solution for entrepreneurs entering the booming cryptocurrency market.
